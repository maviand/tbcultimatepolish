Azeroth Reborn: World Content & Longevity

Classic Azeroth should not become obsolete at level 70. This initiative revitalizes the Eastern Kingdoms and Kalimdor by integrating them into the endgame progression through challenging content, meaningful rewards, and systems consistent with TBC's design. The goal is to make the entire world feel alive and relevant throughout the expansion, a living history that continues to shape our present.

The cornerstone of this initiative is re-tuning Classic raids and dungeons as optional Level 70 Heroic content. Accessed via quests from the Keepers of Time, this system provides a deep well of repeatable endgame content, alternative gearing paths, and a treasure trove of cosmetic and prestige rewards for dedicated champions.

Heroic Raids: Legends Revisited

Molten Core (10-Player)

  • Garr's adds pulse with increased AoE damage and explode on death.
  • Majordomo's lieutenants now reflect certain spell schools, forcing target swaps.
  • Ragnaros's Sons of Flame spawn faster and leave pools of lava.

Blackwing Lair (25-Player)

  • Razorgore's eggs are more resistant and spawn elite Dragonkin if not destroyed quickly.
  • Vaelastrasz's Burning Adrenaline is more lethal and spreads to a nearby player if not dispelled.
  • Nefarian's class calls are more complex, sometimes targeting multiple classes at once.

Ahn'Qiraj (25-Player)

  • The Prophet Skeram creates more illusions and they persist longer.
  • C'Thun's Dark Glare is faster and tentacles inside his stomach are more numerous.
  • Ouro's Sand Blast has a shorter cast time, demanding quicker reactions.

Naxxramas (25-Player)

  • Patchwerk's Hateful Strikes require a three-tank rotation.
  • Grobbulus's poison clouds spread faster and wider, demanding constant movement.
  • Kel'Thuzad's adds are stronger and his Frost Blasts can chain to multiple targets.

To bridge the gap between Outland and Azeroth, new systems are introduced to encourage players to engage with the entire world, creating a single, cohesive endgame experience.

Emissary Quest Chains üìú

Emissaries in Shattrath offer daily and weekly quests sending players back to Heroic Classic Dungeons and Raids. This provides a reliable, alternative source of Badges of Justice, reputation with both TBC and Classic factions, and valuable supply caches.

World PvP Invasions üî•

Dynamic, server-wide events erupt periodically in contested zones. Repel Fel Invasions in Stranglethorn Vale or fight back Scourge Uprisings in the Plaguelands. Participation grants Honor and event-specific currency for powerful PvP rewards.

Paragon Reputations & Faction Revival üåü

To keep old-world factions relevant, a "Paragon" reputation system is introduced. For every 10,000 reputation earned past Exalted, players receive a unique Paragon Cache containing themed rewards. This provides long-term goals and revitalizes the Classic economy.

Argent Dawn

Continue the fight against the Scourge.

Paragon Cache Contains: Chance at [Recipe: Greater Shadow Protection Potion], [Pattern: Dawn's Embrace] (TBC-level Shadow Resist cloth gear), and a rare chance for the [Spectral Charger's Reins] mount.

Thorium Brotherhood

Fuel the war machine with fire and steel.

Paragon Cache Contains: Chance at [Plans: Adamantite Weightstone], [Pattern: Molten Helm] (TBC-level Fire Resist plate gear), and the unique [Schematic: Field Repair Bot 110G].

Cenarion Circle

Restore the balance of nature.

Paragon Cache Contains: High-level Nature Resist recipes, [Pattern: Living Earth Bindings] (TBC-level leather gear), a chance at the [Enchant: Verdant Sphere] cosmetic weapon illusion, and a rare [Cenarion War Hippogryph] pet.

Zandalar Tribe

Uncover the secrets of the Loa.

Paragon Cache Contains: Unique recipes for powerful stat food, flasks that provide unique buffs (e.g., increased damage against beasts), and cosmetic replicas of the iconic Zul'Gurub armor sets.
