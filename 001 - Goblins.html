The Goblin Initiative: A Proposal for Neutrality

This document presents a comprehensive design for introducing Goblins as a true Neutral race in The Burning Crusade. The core objective is to dynamically address long-standing faction imbalance, invigorate the world economy, and offer unprecedented player agency. By prioritizing profit over politics, the Goblin cartels offer their services to the highest bidder, forever changing the face of the war for Outland.

The Goblin Experience: Birth of an Entrepreneur

Starting Zone: Isle of Kezan (Levels 1-12) üåã

Players begin their journey amidst the polluted, hyper-capitalist paradise of Kezan. The narrative kicks off not with a noble calling, but with the cataclysmic eruption of Mount Kajaro. The starting experience is a frantic, hilarious escape from the island, driven by one motivation: saving your own skin (and your investment portfolio).

Narrative Arc:

  • The Boom: Witness the eruption of Mount Kajaro. The first quest is simply "Get Out Alive!", setting a tone of chaotic self-preservation.
  • Hostile Takeover: Key quests involve collecting outstanding debts, "repossessing" essential hardware for an escape rocket, and sabotaging rival entrepreneurs' escape plans.
  • The Gallywix Betrayal: Secure a spot on Trade Prince Gallywix's personal yacht, only to be enslaved and forced to pay an exorbitant fee for your own rescue before being shipwrecked.

The Allegiance Choice: The Lost Isles (Levels 12-20) üèùÔ∏è

On the Lost Isles, stranded Goblins quickly find themselves caught between Alliance and Horde forces, both of whom see the Goblins' technical expertise as a valuable asset. This zone serves as the crucible where players make their permanent faction choice.

Narrative Arc:

  • The First Offer: Emissaries from both factions arrive, offering rescue and resources in exchange for loyalty. Players undertake introductory quests for both sides, getting a feel for their ethos.
  • A Business Proposal: The culminating quest, "Signing the Contract," forces the player to permanently align with either the Horde or the Alliance. This choice determines their capital city, questlines, and reputation paths for the rest of their journey. The decision is framed as the biggest business deal of their lives.

Goblin Society: Commerce & Chaos

Major Cartels & Capitals üèôÔ∏è

Goblin society is fractured into several rival Trade Cartels, each with its own philosophy.

  • The Bilgewater Cartel (Playable): Led by the opulent and ruthless Trade Prince Gallywix. Having lost their neutrality in the shipwreck, they align with a major faction out of necessity. Their capital is the sprawling, industrial port of Bilgewater Harbor in Azshara.
  • The Steamwheedle Cartel (Neutral): The largest and most established cartel, controlling cities like Gadgetzan, Booty Bay, Everlook, and Ratchet. They are the architects of true neutrality and the engine of the cross-faction economy.

The Neutral Trade Network ‚öñÔ∏è

While individual Goblins pledge allegiance, the Steamwheedle Cartel remains strictly neutral. Cities like Gadgetzan and Booty Bay are expanded into true sanctuaries, offering unique services and housing the revolutionary Cross-Faction Bazaar.

Cross-Faction Bazaar Mechanics:

  • Hosted only in Gadgetzan, run by the Steamwheedle Cartel.
  • Only Goblin characters (of either faction) can post auctions and place buy orders.
  • Characters of any race can purchase items, breaking economic barriers.
  • The Cartel takes a hefty "convenience fee" (e.g., 20% cut) on all transactions, creating a massive gold sink to help regulate the server economy.
