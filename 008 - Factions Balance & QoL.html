Faction Balance & Quality of Life

This addendum focuses on two pillars of a healthy MMO: ensuring fair competition and respecting player time. We propose targeted adjustments to achieve better faction parity while preserving identity, alongside significant, optional quality-of-life upgrades that modernize the user experience without sacrificing the classic TBC feel.

Faction Balance: Addressing Disparities ⚜️

The goal is to achieve better faction parity through targeted, subtle adjustments to racials and class interactions, while carefully preserving the unique identity of each faction. No homogenization—just balance.

Shaman & Paladin Parity

Alliance Paladin Buffs

Alliance Paladins gain access to Seal of the Martyr via Retribution talents, an ability identical in function to the Horde's Seal of Blood, ensuring competitive PvE damage potential. Additionally, talents are added to make Blessings of Might/Wisdom party-wide auras, reducing buff maintenance.

Horde Shaman Buffs

To bring Horde's utility closer to Alliance's Blessing of Salvation, Tranquil Air Totem's threat reduction is increased. Furthermore, Heroism/Bloodlust now also grants a 10% mana cost reduction for its duration, adding a valuable efficiency component to the powerful haste cooldown.
